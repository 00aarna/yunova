About Yunoa

Our Mission

Yunoa is a learning initiative started by Aarna Sharma, focused on experimenting with small businesses. We identify challenges, propose solutions, and document our learning journey to help other businesses grow and improve.

Why Yunoa?

We believe that every small business has the potential for growth with the right guidance and innovative solutions. By starting small, we gain insights and share our findings with the world.

Our Founder

Aarna Sharma, a passionate 16-year-old, is diving deep into the world of business consulting to help others. She documents her findings and experiences as she explores various small businesses.
