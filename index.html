About Us

Yunova is a passion-led experimental initiative started by Aarna Sharma, a 16-year-old student exploring the world of business strategy and consulting. This is not a firm — it's a sandbox for ideas. Yunova studies small, local businesses, identifies pain points, proposes creative improvements, and blogs about the outcomes.

The aim is to build a growing repository of case studies and insights for students, early entrepreneurs, and curious minds.
